Are you a footie fan? Here are some impressive facts about the success of Madrid's football teams.

Madrid is home to two of the most successful football clubs in Spain: Real Madrid and Atlético Madrid. These two clubs have a fierce rivalry and their matches, known as "El Derbi," are some of the most competitive in Spain given theyre from the same area

Real Madrid, also known as "Los Blancos," was founded in 1902 and has won a record 34 La Liga titles (Translated to The league in Spanish) and 13 UEFA Champions League titles. The team plays its home matches at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, which has a capacity of over 80,000 spectators. Pretty large in comparision to Wembley

Atlético Madrid, also known as "Los Colchoneros," was founded in 1903 just a year later and has won 11 La Liga titles and 3 UEFA Europa League titles. The team plays its home matches at the Wanda Metropolitano Stadium, which has a capacity of over 68,000 spectators.

As well as Real Madrid and Atlético Madrid, Madrid also has several other football clubs such as Getafe CF, CD Leganés, and Rayo Vallecano.
