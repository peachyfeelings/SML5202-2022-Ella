Easy pancakes!

Fancy a bit of a more substantial breakfast for you and your family to enjoy? This easy pancake recipe is completely fool proof to ensure perfect pancakes every time!(Well, as long as you follow the recipe)

Make this for your friends, family, or even just yourself if youre feeling particularly hungry!

Ingredients

Makes 12 pancakes

  • 100 plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil, or a bit extra for perfect frying!
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar, or to taste
  • LEmon wedges to serve (optional)
  • Caster sugar to serve (optional)

Preparation

  • Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  • Set a medium frying pan or crÃªpe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  • When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  • Add the lemon wedges and caster sugar, or even add in your own twist by frying some bacon and adding some maple syrup too!
  • Serve!
