Roasted Cauliflower Recipe

Cauliflower is one of the greatest vegetables to roast, transforming from dry and tasteless to soft, sweet and juicy with terrific caramelised edges.

It also requires minimal work to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp each salt and pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1 tbsp parsley , roughly chopped, for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C/425°F (200°C fan).
  2. Toss cauliflower in a large bowl with oil, salt, pepper and garlic if using.
  3. Spread on tray, and roast for 20 minutes.
  4. Flip cauliflower, roast a further 5 minutes until you have golden edges and it’s soft all the way through.
  5. Serve immediately, sprinkled with parsley if using.

Easy pancakes!

Fancy a bit of a more substantial breakfast for you and your family to enjoy? This easy pancake recipe is completely fool proof to ensure perfect pancakes every time!(Well, as long as you follow the recipe)

Make this for your friends, family, or even just yourself if youre feeling particularly hungry!

Ingredients

Makes 12 pancakes

  • 100 plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil, or a bit extra for perfect frying!
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar, or to taste
  • LEmon wedges to serve (optional)
  • Caster sugar to serve (optional)

Preparation

  • Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  • Set a medium frying pan or crêpe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  • When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  • Add the lemon wedges and caster sugar, or even add in your own twist by frying some bacon and adding some maple syrup too!
  • Serve!
