Easy pancakes!

Fancy a bit of a more substantial breakfast for you and your family to enjoy? This easy pancake recipe is completely fool proof to ensure perfect pancakes every time!(Well, as long as you follow the recipe)

Make this for your friends, family, or even just yourself if youre feeling particularly hungry!

Ingredients

Makes 12 pancakes

  • 10 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 3 oz fresh mozzarella, cut to size to match cherry tomatoes
  • 6 basil leaves, chiffonade
  • 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil, or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar, or to taste
  • 2 healthy pinches of Kosher salt, or to taste
  • 6-8 grinds of pepper, or to taste

Preparation

  • Pepare the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil as specified, and place in a medium-sized bowl.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Add extra virgin olive oil and balsamic. Toss to coat.
  • Adjust seasoning as needed.
  • Serve!
