Spanish Idioms

Common Spanish Idioms

Spanish idioms, also known as "proverbios" or simply "idiomas" in Spanish are phrases that convey a meaning that is different from the literal meaning of the words used. They are an important part of the Spanish language and culture, and understanding them can help improve your communication and comprehension.
